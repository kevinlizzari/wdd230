Site Name: Milano Chamber of Commerce

Selected because it represents the city of Milano (city where I have lived all my life)

Site Purpose:

  • Provide a platform for local businesses to connect and collaborate.
  • Offer resources and information for economic and community development in Milano.

Scenarios:

  • How can I become a member of the Milano Chamber of Commerce and what are the benefits of membership?
  • Is there a directory of local businesses available on the Milano Chamber of Commerce website?
  • Can I find information about business permits and regulations for starting a business in Milano on this website?

Color Scheme:

  • Primary Color: #3498db Usage:
    • Headings (h1, h2, h3, etc.)
    • Accents and highlights for buttons, links, and important elements
    • Borders and separator lines to provide emphasis
  • Secondary Color: White #f5f5f5 Usage:
    • Background color for the overall page
    • Background for sections or cards to provide contrast with content
    • General background for a clean and unobtrusive look

Typography:

  • Font: Mooli
    • Body text
  • Body Text Font: Roboto Medium 500
    • Headings (h1, h2, h3, etc.)
